I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Michican State University, starting in Fall 2024. Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral researcher at University of Southern California. I obtained my doctoral degree from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.

I have fully funded RA positions and I am looking for self-motivated students. Contact me if you are interested. Details can be found here.
