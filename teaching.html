CSE 801B Fall 2024
Introduction to Data Mining

An introduction to the core principles and algorithms behind discovering patterns in large datasets. Students learn to formulate data mining problems, select appropriate methods, and critically evaluate results. The course balances theory with hands-on implementation in Python.

Data preprocessing Classification Clustering Association rules Dimensionality reduction Evaluation & validation

CSE 849 Spring 2025 · Spring 2026
Deep Learning

A graduate-level course covering the theory and practice of modern deep learning. Topics span from foundational neural network concepts to cutting-edge architectures. Students complete a substantial research project applying deep learning to a problem of their choice.

Neural networks Optimization CNNs Transformers Generative models Graph neural networks Self-supervised learning
