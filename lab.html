DominoAI Lab

Domain Knowledge + AI  ·  Michigan State University

The DominoAI Lab studies how to incorporate domain knowledge, from physics, causality, and symbolic reasoning, into deep learning systems to make them more data-efficient, interpretable, and reliable. Our work spans knowledge-augmented deep learning, probabilistic graphical models, computer vision, and AI for health.

We are actively recruiting PhD students and research interns. If you are interested in joining the lab, please send your CV and a brief statement of interest to cuizijun@msu.edu.
